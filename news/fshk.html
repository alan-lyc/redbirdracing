FSHK An alliance formed by 3 formula student teams in Hong Kong

Hong Kong Inter-University Formula Student Alliance is an alliance that is formed by 3 formula student teams in Hong Kong. Namely, PolyU E-formula Racing Team (PERT), HKUST Red Bird Racing (HKUST RBR), and HKU Racing (HKUR). We fabricate and drive our small-scale formula-style race car to compete in Formula Student events around the world.

Our aims of Triple A

  • Attract young talents to be Formula Student
  • Able to not just drive, but also build
  • Attention grabber from the local racing sector
